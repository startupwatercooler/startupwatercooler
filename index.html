About

Server Side Solvers is a group of friendly people from in and around Ghent that are working on projects and startups, love exchanging ideas about them, and meet up irregularly to do just that over a couple of nice Belgian beers.

We arrange meetups and exchange ideas and other content through the Server Side Solvers Facebook group

There are no rules whatsoever, but for some reason these have been constants so far:

  • We always seem to meet in Manteca.
  • We always seem to drink mostly Augustijn.
  • We always seem to meet on Thursdays.
  • 'beertrepreneurs' and 'server side solvers' are interchangable.
  • There's always that one guy present that doesn't speak any Dutch beyond 'Augustijn', so we speak English all night.
